We specialize in the meticulous restoration of automotive history. By blending classic aesthetics with modern precision, we ensure that every legendary machine we touch is brought back to its ultimate glory.

Explore Collection

From a Small Garage to India's Premier Facility

Founded in 1985 by master craftsman **Rajesh Mehta**, Vintage Restore began as a modest two-person garage. What started as a passion project soon grew into a trusted name among classic car enthusiasts across the nation.

Today, our 5,000 sq.ft facility houses **12 expert craftsmen** who have collectively restored over 500 classic automobiles. We've earned the trust of collectors worldwide, featured in prestigious concours events.

"Every car has a story. We're here to help it live forever."

— Rajesh Mehta, Founder

How long does a typical restoration take?

Restoration timelines vary depending on the scope of work. A full concours restoration typically takes 12-18 months, while partial restorations or mechanical work can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Quick response:
24h

Do you source original parts?

Yes, we have a global network of parts suppliers and specialize in sourcing authentic period-correct components. When original parts aren't available, we use high-quality reproductions that meet original specifications.

Original Parts Global Network Quality Assured

What is the cost range for restoration?

Costs vary based on the car's condition and restoration scope. Basic mechanical restoration starts from ₹1.8L, full body restoration from ₹2.4L, and complete concours restorations can range from ₹5L to ₹15L+. We provide detailed quotes after inspection.
